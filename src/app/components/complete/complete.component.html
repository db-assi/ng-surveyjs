Perimenopause means ‘around the menopause,’ and typically starts in a woman’s 40s. It is the time when signs and symptoms of falling oestrogen levels begin to show. Most commonly irregular periods or hot flushes. Perimenopause lasts until 12 months after your final menstrual period. For over half of women, the perimenopause stage lasts for over 7 years.
